What is Parcel?

Parcel is a YSWS where hackers build hardware projects and exchange them with each other through a shared marketplace. Participants start by designing a project (schematics, PCB, CAD files, BOM,etc ), tracking their time through Dev-Logs and Lapse, and earning “Stamps” (1 Stamp = $1) based on hours and project quality. They then use these Stamps to manufacture and build their project in real life, continuing to document their progress and earn additional Stamps. Once complete, the project is listed on the Parcel shop with its repo, documentation, working prototype, and demo (photos/videos), and is priced in stamps based on its build cost. Other hackers can use their Stamps to receive these projects, with PCBs and 3D prints(through #printing-legion) shipped to them along with grants to purchase remaining components, allowing them to build the project themselves, again track time, get stamps and continue the cycle.
